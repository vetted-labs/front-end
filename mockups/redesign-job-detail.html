Job Description

About the Role

Nexus Protocol is building the next generation of decentralized financial infrastructure. We're looking for a Senior Solidity Engineer to lead the development of our core smart contract suite, including lending pools, automated market makers, and cross-chain bridge contracts. You'll work closely with our security team and auditors to deliver battle-tested code that secures hundreds of millions in TVL.

This is a high-impact role where you'll shape the architecture of protocols that power real economic activity on-chain. You'll have significant ownership over technical decisions and direct influence on our product roadmap.

What You'll Do
  • Design and implement core DeFi protocol smart contracts (Solidity 0.8+) including lending markets, liquidity pools, and governance modules
  • Lead smart contract architecture decisions, optimizing for gas efficiency, upgradeability, and composability across multiple EVM chains
  • Collaborate with external auditors (Trail of Bits, OpenZeppelin) on security reviews, writing comprehensive test suites with >95% coverage
  • Build and maintain deployment pipelines using Foundry and Hardhat, with automated testing on forked mainnets
  • Mentor junior engineers on Solidity best practices, common vulnerability patterns, and EVM internals
  • Contribute to protocol governance proposals and tokenomics modeling
Requirements
  • 4+ years of professional Solidity development with production deployments on Ethereum mainnet
  • Deep understanding of EVM internals, gas optimization patterns, and common vulnerability classes (reentrancy, flash loan attacks, oracle manipulation)
  • Experience with DeFi primitives: AMMs, lending protocols, yield aggregators, or bridge contracts
  • Proficiency with Foundry (forge, cast, anvil) and/or Hardhat development frameworks
  • Strong understanding of formal verification tools (Certora, Echidna, Slither) and invariant testing
  • Excellent written communication skills for technical documentation and governance proposals
  • Comfort working asynchronously in a distributed, remote-first team across time zones

Required Skills
