How Reputation Works

1

Complete reviews -- Each candidate review you submit is evaluated for accuracy by comparing your assessment against the consensus of other guild experts.

2

Build consistency -- Your consistency score reflects how reliably your reviews align with outcomes. Experts who maintain steady quality earn higher reputation over time.

3

Stay timely -- Completing reviews before their deadlines boosts your timeliness metric. Late reviews reduce your score and may affect job assignment priority.

4

Earn community trust -- Endorsements from other experts, successful dispute resolutions, and governance participation all contribute to your community trust score.

5

Unlock tiers -- As your composite score increases, you advance through reward tiers. Each tier unlocks higher multipliers, priority assignments, and governance weight on-chain.
